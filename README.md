# WINNA-haptic-feedback-based-free-throw-sleeve-
A haptic feedback sleeve designed to perfect a basketball free throw. It uses IMUs on the sleeve to capture the precise mechanics of a player's shot, which are elbow angle, wrist position, and release speed. Multi-directional vibration motors then deliver instant, intuitive cues that guide you into the ideal form. It’s like a coach's hand gently steering your own. Our AI algorithm adapts to your skill level, making every repetition a step toward mastery.We're democratizing elite, 1:1 coaching and packing thousands of hours of expert instruction into every single shot to ensure that those without coaches still have an opportunity to perfect their game.
